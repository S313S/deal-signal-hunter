# Deal Diagnosis

Use this reference when the client stage, intent, or risk is unclear.

## Stage Map

| Stage | Signals | Risk | Best next action |
|---|---|---|---|
| 试探 | asks "can this be done", gives little material | free consulting spiral | ask for goal, input, output, success criteria |
| 预算评估 | asks total price, basis, third-party costs | single dead price too early | give tiered range and what changes the price |
| 信任验证 | asks examples, process, why you can do it | over-explaining tools | show one relevant case, workflow, and next small step |
| 压价 | says optimize price, early stage, long-term chance | price cut without scope cut | trade price for scope, not dignity |
| 拖延 | promises materials, no follow-up, vague timing | unpaid work and lost schedule | set deadline, starter fee, and re-quote rule |
| 分成替代 | proposes future revenue share instead of current fee | future uncertainty replaces real work | separate build fee from later growth share |
| 准备成交 | asks contract, payment, delivery, timeline | unclear scope causes later conflict | confirm scope, payment node, revision limits |

## Fast Diagnosis Questions

Ask at most one if needed:

- "你现在最想让我判断的是能不能成交、怎么报价，还是下一条怎么回？"
- "这个客户目前有没有明确预算或付款动作？"
- "你已经交付了什么，哪些还没有正式收费？"

## Risk Language

Use neutral business language:

- "这不是坏客户信号，但说明项目优先级还不够高。"
- "可以继续培养，但不适合继续做深度免费交付。"
- "现在要从证明能力切换到确认付款门槛。"
- "分成可以作为后续合作，不建议替代当前基础建设费。"
